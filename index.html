Values

Aesthetics

Gone are the days of customers buying products that just worked properly, but looked horrible. It's time that our products are not only beautiful externally, but even down to the little details like proper code syntax.

User Experience

From start to finish, it's imperative that the product's UX be considered at every step and turn in order to create the best product possible.

Accessibility

As contributors to any product that users interact with, it is our responsibility to do our utmost to ensure that they are built with every user in mind.
